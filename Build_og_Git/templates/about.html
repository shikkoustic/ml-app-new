About Us

Vayu: is a student led project that aims to forecast air quality and provide air quality insight in the simplest and the most accurate way possible.

We use satellite based data and our advanced machine learning models to provide you with the air quality insights.

Currently based in Delhi, we look to advance this venture to other cities of the country too.

*Our insights are subjected to errors, the forecasted data has slight error margins
